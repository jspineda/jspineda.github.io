Research Program

I work at the intersection of stellar astrophysics and exoplanetary science investigating the different ways that magnetic fields influence observable phenomnena in astronomy.

For insight into some of my current research activities please have a look below. You can also find an up to date list of publications here.

Current research programs:

  • Star-planet interactions
  • Far Ultraviolet M-dwarf Evolution Survey (FUMES)
  • Brown dwarf aurorae
  • Simulations of (sub)stellar environments

Current and past graduate student mentees include Dr. Girish Duvvuri and Anna Zuckerman.
